About CodeHelp

codehelp's mission is to make coding education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience. Codehelp offers a variety of courses on topics such as web development, data science, and machine learning. Each course is taught by a team of experienced engineers who are passionate about teaching. Codehelp also provides resources to help students learn, including practice problems, coding challenges, and a platform for asking questions and getting help from other students and instructors. Codehelp is a great option for anyone who wants to learn to code and offers a variety of courses.
